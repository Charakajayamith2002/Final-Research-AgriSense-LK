About Market Ranking

Our AI model analyses multiple factors to rank the best markets for your produce:

  • Real-time & predicted market prices per kg
  • Distance & transport cost from your location
  • Net advantage (revenue minus all costs)
  • Buyer vs. Seller role optimisation
  • Quantity-based bulk pricing analysis
  • Market accessibility & route efficiency
Price predictions use machine learning trained on historical Sri Lanka market data.
